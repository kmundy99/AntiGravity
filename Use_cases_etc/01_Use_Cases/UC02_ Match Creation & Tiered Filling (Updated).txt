UC02: Match Creation & Tiered Filling (Updated)
1. Visibility & "Ghost" Notifications
●​ The Notification Rule: A user only receives a Push/SMS if Preference_Notify == True.
●​ The Feed Rule: If Preference_Notify == False, the user receives no alert, but the match
appears in their "Available Matches" feed.
●​ Cumulative Visibility: When a match escalates from Circle 1 to Circle 2, Circle 1 retains
visibility. The "Available" pool only grows; it never shrinks until the match is full.
●​ The "Filled" State: Once the Required_Count is met, the match is removed from all
feeds. It only appears in the "Scheduled Matches" tab for the Organizer and the 3
Confirmed Players.
2. The Waitlist & Dropping Out
●​ Waitlist Capacity: Exactly 1 spot per match.
●​ Auto-Promotion: If a confirmed player drops out:
1.​ The Waitlist player is automatically promoted to Confirmed & Notified ( bypasses
all Preference_Notify settings).
2.​ The Match remains Hidden from feeds (because it is still technically full).
●​ Vacancy Logic: If a confirmed player drops out and the Waitlist is empty:
1.​ The match status reverts to Filling.
2.​ It reappears in the feeds of the highest Circle reached (and all preceding Circles).
3.​ Notifications are re-sent to those with active preferences.
3. Organizer Overrides & Direct Recruiting
●​ Manual Management: The Organizer has "God Mode" for their match—they can
add/remove any user or delete the match entirely at any time.
●​ The "1-Hour Warning": At $T-60$ minutes, if Confirmed_Count < Required_Count:
1.​ A "Match Underfilled" alert is sent to all confirmed players and the organizer.
2.​ The Organizer can trigger Direct Recruiting. This bypasses all
Preference_Notify settings (it is treated as a direct "Urgent" message) to find a
last-minute sub.
4. Race Conditions
●​ Timestamp Priority: In a collision for the last slot, the earliest Server_Timestamp wins.
●​ Fallback: The "loser" of the collision is automatically offered the Waitlist spot if it is
vacant; otherwise, they receive a "Match Full" notification.
