UC03_Organizers_Dashboard
Status: Draft
Version: 1.1
Primary Flow:
1.​Access: Organizer opens the "My Matches" tab and selects a
specific match.
2.​Roster Visibility: System displays the names and status of the 4
slots + 1 waitlist slot.
3.​Tier Management: * System displays: "Currently inviting Circle 1.
Next: Circle 2 in 01:45:00."
○​ Organizer has the option to click "Invite Circle 2 Now" to
bypass the timer.
4.​Manual Player Removal:
○​ Organizer selects "Remove" next to a player's name.
○​ Note Field: A modal appears allowing the Organizer to input
a reason (e.g., "Replacing with a family member").
○​ Execution: System removes the player and sends them a
notification containing the Organizer's message.
5.​Edit/Cancel: * Edit: If Organizer edits Time/Location, all
Confirmed and Waitlisted players receive an update notification.
○​ Cancel: Organizer deletes the match. A modal appears for a
"Cancellation Message." All players are notified with the
reason, and the match is removed from all feeds.
6.​Direct Recruit:
○​ Organizer selects "Direct Recruit."
○​ Organizer selects specific target players or an entire
Circle.
○​ Urgent Override: System sends notifications that bypass the
players' "Notify" preferences to ensure the message is seen.
