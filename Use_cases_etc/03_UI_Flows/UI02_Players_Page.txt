Page 02: The Players Page
1. UI/UX Flow & Logic

●​ The Directory View: * A list of all players in your Circles (Circle 1, 2, 3).
○​ Each player card shows: Name, NTRP, Gender, and a "Proximity"
tag (e.g., "2 miles away").
●​ The "Availability & Heatmap" Feature:
○​ This is the feature you described. When you select a player (or look
at their summary), the app shows a mini-calendar view for that
person.
○​ Logic: It doesn't need to show private details, just "Busy" or "Match
Scheduled" blocks.
○​ Strategic Utility: If you see three players from Circle 1 are all "Free"
on Thursday at 10:00 AM, that’s your signal to create a match.
●​ Direct Recruiting:
○​ A "Direct Message/Invite" button next to each player. As we
discussed, this bypasses notification preferences for urgent needs.
●​ Search & Filter:
○​ Filter by NTRP range (e.g., "Show me all 4.0 players") or Gender.
2. The "Unified Calendar" View

You mentioned wanting to see the Available Matches organized by calendar.
●​ The Interface: A vertical or horizontal weekly calendar.
●​ Empty Slots: The calendar shows "Open Slots" where matches could
happen, or where invitations are currently pending.
●​ Comparison: Your personal "Scheduled Matches" appear in a different
color (e.g., Green) and "Available Invitations" in another (e.g., Blue).
