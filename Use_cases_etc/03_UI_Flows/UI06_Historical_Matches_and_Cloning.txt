UI06: Historical Matches & Cloning
Status: Draft | Version: 1.1
1. The "Past Matches" View
●​ Trigger: Matches automatically move from the "Upcoming" list to the "History" list 24
hours after the scheduled start time.
●​ Entry Point: A "Past" or "History" sub-tab within the Matches tab.
●​ Card Layout: * Summary of Date and Location.
○​ Names/Avatars of the 4 players who were on the final roster.
●​ Primary Action: A prominent "Schedule Rematch" button.
2. The Cloning Flow (Logic) When a user clicks "Schedule Rematch":
●​ Step 1: The Draft State. The app opens a new Match Creation page and pre-fills the
following from the old match:
○​ Activity Type: (e.g., Tennis).
○​ Location: (e.g., Lexington High).
○​ The Roster: The 3 other players are automatically added to the invitation list.
●​ Step 2: Date/Time Selection. * The Date/Time fields are highlighted for the organizer to
update.
●​ Step 3: Automated Availability Check. * As soon as the organizer picks a new time,
the app performs a background check on the 3 pre-filled players.
○​ If any are "Busy" (already in another match), a warning icon appears: "Note:
Joost appears to have another match at this time."
●​ Step 4: Activation. * When the organizer clicks "Send," invitations are sent to those
specific individuals. Note: This bypasses "Circle" logic as it is a direct re-invite.
3. Reference Access & Chat
●​ Read-Only Chat: Users can click into a historical match to view the chat log.
○​ Behavior: The thread becomes "Read-Only" 24 hours after match completion.
○​ Value: Allows players to retrieve past info (e.g., "Which gate code did we use?").
●​ Privacy: Only the original 4 players and the organizer have permission to view the
historical record and its associated chat.
